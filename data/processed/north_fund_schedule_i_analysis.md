## Executive Summary

Analysis of 142 grant recipients across the North Fund's 2023-2024 Schedule I filings found **ZERO grants to any child safety, age verification, digital childhood, or tech policy advocacy organization**. The fund's $59.2M in grants went overwhelmingly to progressive state-level civic engagement, reproductive rights, and voter mobilization organizations. This extends the Arabella network negative finding to three entities.

**Combined Arabella Analysis:** Across **NVF + STF + North Fund**, a total of **3,117 grants** totaling approximately **$1.56 billion** have now been analyzed, with **zero dollars** going to any organization related to child online safety, age verification, or the App Store Accountability Act.

## 7. Conclusions

### What This Establishes

1. **North Fund does NOT fund** any child safety, age verification, or tech policy organization via Schedule I grants
2. **Combined NVF + STF + North Fund:** 3,117 grants totaling ~$1.56 billion with ZERO child safety funding
3. **Circular Arabella flow confirmed:** North Fund returns ~$5.3M annually to STF, creating NVF в†’ STF в†’ North Fund в†’ STF circular transfer
4. **The Arabella "Technology and Innovation" category** ($2.66M from North Fund) goes to clean energy (Rewiring America $2.4M), conservation (LCV $7.5K), and Windward Fund ($250K) вЂ” NOT tech policy or digital safety
5. **Chain of opacity:** North Fund grants primarily to other 501(c)(4)s, extending the donor-concealment chain

### What Remains Unknown

1. **Windward Fund** Schedule I ($311M revenue) вЂ” not yet analyzed
2. **Hopewell Fund** Schedule I ($114M grants) вЂ” not yet analyzed
3. Whether any Arabella entity operates DCA as a **fiscal sponsorship** (would not appear on any Schedule I)
4. Whether NVF's $36.7M in **lobbying expenditures** (Part IX) fund child safety advocacy
